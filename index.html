About · the human behind the mammoth

I watched AI take the gigs I used to win. So I learned to build with it.

Jack Fentress Owner & AIBMM Executive Coach

I started out as a freelance marketer and copywriter. Brand guides, landing pages, lead magnets, campaigns, etc. Mostly for sustainability and ESG companies, back in 2021. Then one day I opened ChatGPT for the first time, typed a prompt, and watched it do a version of my job in about four seconds. I'm not going to lie to you: that was a weird afternoon.

I could've dug in, fought it, and resisted the AI movement. Instead I got curious. And the deeper I went, the more familiar it felt. Reverse-engineering, workflows, big-picture vision, and efficiency applications. All skills I had already developed. The difference? Better tools. Same thinking.

So while everyone was bracing for AI to end the world, I started learning to drive it. And I found the gap: businesses need someone who gets how their operations actually run, then builds the systems that help their growth compound.

That's Jolly Mammoth. We advise and build, starting with your business, not the tech. If you're nervous about what AI means for your company, I get it. I was on the wrong end of it years ago. Today, my goal is to help business owners feel confident to take on the shifting business landscape.
